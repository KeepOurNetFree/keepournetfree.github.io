Take Action



Contact Your Congressperson

Send an email or call your representative and either show them appreciation for opposing this bill or chastise them for supporting it.



Sign the Petition

There is a petition on whitehouse.gov to reinstate the FCC's broadband privacy rules.



Be Active on Social Media

Our rally point and main hub is over at /r/KeepOurNetFree

Help us trend on twitter. Help us get the word out with the hashtag #KeepOurNetFree.



Donate

We do not take donations, but if you feel so inclined you may donate your money to one of the following organizations fighting for Internet freedoms.

Electronic Frontier Foundation

American Civil Liberties Union

Center for Democracy

Fight for the Future

The Onion Router



How else can I help?

Do you have marketing or web development skills? Message the mods on Reddit or join our IRC and Discord. Spread the word! Don't just keep this to Reddit or Imgur - spread it far and wide. Share this on social media. Tell your friends, family, coworkers. We can always fight against this bill and for net neutrality.





KeepOurNetFree is a grassroots movement dedicated to promoting online privacy. We do not take donations and are staffed entirely by volunteers.

KeepOurNetFree, Private Internet Access, Snoonet, and affiliates aim to protect the Integrity of the Internet.
