Keep Our Net Free



Background

KeepOurNetFree is a group dedicated to fighting the most recent resolution passed by the Senate and House - namely, S.J. Res. 34/H.J. Res. 86, that kills a number of FCC regulations that are integral to the privacy rights of all Internet users and U.S. citizens.

Among other things, this resolution allows ISPs to sell their customers' internet browsing history and confidential data to the highest bidder without the knowledge or consent of their customers. This is an issue that affects every U.S. citizen and every Internet user.

What does this bill do?

This was the first step to allow Internet service providers (ISPs) to sell private browsing information and app data usage to third parties. What's worse, they can manipulate your Internet experience with ad injections and search redirections. This is a massive violation of privacy and should be a major concern to all citizens.

The resolution has just passed the House of Representatives and is on its way to being signed by the President. That doesn't mean, however, that there is nothing you can do about it.
